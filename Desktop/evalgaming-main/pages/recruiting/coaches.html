Overview of Eval for Coaches

Eval is a platform designed to support coaches by providing a streamlined way to evaluate, track, and enhance player performance. It combines data-driven insights, intuitive interfaces, and customizable tools, making it easier for coaches to make informed decisions, manage teams, and foster player development.

Key Features for Coaches:

Player Performance Evaluation

  • Input, track, and visualize player performance metrics
  • Customizable evaluation criteria

Leaderboards and Rankings

  • Real-time insights into team standings
  • Customizable ranking metrics

Team Management Tools

  • Detailed team rosters and profiles
  • Schedule and practice management

Analytics and Reports

  • Detailed performance analytics
  • Shareable progress reports
