Overview of Eval for High School Athletes and Recruitment

Eval is a platform designed to elevate high school athletes' visibility and help them showcase their skills for college recruiting. By combining performance tracking, rankings, and shareable reports, Eval empowers athletes to stand out to college recruiters while giving coaches the tools to highlight their players' achievements effectively.

Player Performance Tracking

  • Input, track, and showcase individual performance metrics
  • Customizable evaluation criteria for specific strengths

Leaderboards and Rankings

  • Real-time leaderboards across schools or leagues
  • Customizable metrics to demonstrate unique skills

Recruiting Profiles

  • Comprehensive player profiles with stats and highlights
  • Shareable links for recruiters

Analytics and Reports

  • Advanced performance analytics
  • Shareable progress reports for recruiters
